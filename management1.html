📘 UNIT 1: BUSINESS & MANAGEMENT (EXAM READY)

1. Business

Definition:
Business is an economic activity involving production and sale of goods/services to earn profit and satisfy customer needs.
Business is a value-creating activity. It converts raw materials into useful products and delivers them to consumers.
👉 Important Line: Business connects production, distribution, and consumption.

2. Nature of Business

  • Economic activity
  • Production and sale
  • Continuity
  • Profit motive
  • Risk and uncertainty
  • Legal operation
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Social responsibility
Business is dynamic and profit-oriented but must also focus on ethics and customer satisfaction.
👉 Important Line: Profit is not the only goal; customer satisfaction is equally important.

3. Management

Management is the process of planning, organizing, staffing, directing, and controlling resources to achieve goals.
Management ensures proper use of resources. It balances efficiency (less cost) and effectiveness (goal achievement).
👉 Important Line: Management balances efficiency and effectiveness.

4. Characteristics of Management

  • Universal
  • Goal-oriented
  • Continuous
  • Group activity
  • Dynamic
  • Science and Art
  • Professional
  • Multidisciplinary
Management adapts to changes in environment like technology and competition.

5. Functions of Management

Planning → Organizing → Staffing → Directing → Controlling
  • Planning: Decide goals
  • Organizing: Arrange resources
  • Staffing: Select employees
  • Directing: Guide workers
  • Controlling: Check results
All functions are interrelated and must be performed in sequence.
👉 Trick: WHAT → HOW → WHO → DO → CHECK

6. Managerial Roles

Interpersonal: Figurehead, Leader, Liaison Informational: Monitor, Disseminator, Spokesperson Decisional: Entrepreneur, Disturbance Handler, Resource Allocator, Negotiator
Managers act as leaders, communicators, and decision-makers in organizations.
👉 Important Line: Manager performs multiple roles simultaneously.

7. Evolution of Management Thought

  • Classical: Efficiency focus
  • Behavioral: Human focus
  • System: Organization as system
  • Contingency: Depends on situation
Management ideas evolved from machine-based thinking to human-centered approaches.
👉 Important Line: No one best way (Contingency approach).

8. Technology in Business

  • E-commerce
  • Automation
  • AI
  • Digital marketing
Technology improves efficiency, reduces cost, and increases competitiveness.
👉 Important Line: Technology is the backbone of modern business.

9. Business Environment

Business environment includes all internal and external factors affecting business.
Internal: Employees, Management External: Customers, Suppliers, Government, Technology
Environment is dynamic and uncontrollable, so businesses must adapt.
👉 Important Line: Survival depends on adaptability.

10. Porter’s Five Forces

New Entrants ↑ Suppliers ← Competition → Buyers Substitutes ↓
  • Competition
  • New entrants
  • Substitutes
  • Buyer power
  • Supplier power
Used to analyze competition and profitability in industry.
👉 Strong forces = Low profit 👉 Weak forces = High profit

11. Business & Technology Interface

  • Better decision making
  • Global business
  • Higher efficiency
Technology supports management in improving business performance.
